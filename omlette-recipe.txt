Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - a pinch of salt
 - a pinch of pepper
 - 1 tablesoon of butter

Directions:
 1. Add salt and pepper to egg and mix
 2. Pour butter into a bowl and beat with electric beater for 1 minute
 3. Heat frying pan with little butter.
 4. Add egg mixture and heat on low for 1 minute.
 5. Fold omlette and serve with ketchup.
